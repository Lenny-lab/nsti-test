    Bio

    Pedro Pony is a supporting character in the series. He is the son of Mr. Pony, an optician, and Mummy Pony. More TBA

    Personality

    Pedro Pony is a friendly pony. He is best friends with Danny Dog. He is somewhat smart in his fields of interest, but otherwise appears to be a bit naive and scatterbrained, as shown in some episodes when he gets the wrong fancy dress costume to an occasion, for example, in Danny's Pirate Party when he arrived to a pirate themed party dressed as a cowboy and in Gym Class, he wore a superhero costume instead of his gym kit.

    Lots of times, Pedro gets late to school due to how he likes sleeping. He also likes playing cowboys on some occasions. He is usually lazy and sleeps at daytime in some episodes. He can be shy as seen in the episode, School Play. He also likes superheroes.

    Appearance

    Main Outfit and Appearance

    Pedro is a cute young tan pony with cream-colored spots on his head. He has brown lips, a pink nose, a pair of black glasses, and a tail. He wears a bright yellow outfit and black shoes. He has no visible mane.

    Other Outfits

    Boots

    When Pedro Pony jumps in muddy puddles like everyone else, he wears boots that are purple.

    Swimsuit

    In the episodes The Sandcastle, Swimming Lesson, and Water Park, his swimsuit is light green with horizontal purple stripes on it.

    Trivia

    • In "School Play", Peppa kisses Pedro on the cheek.
    • In "The Eye Test", when Pedro slips into a muddy puddle, he loses his glasses and he can't see very well.
    • He's the "clumsy one" in Peppa's playgroup.
    • He broke his leg in The Hospital and Christmas at the Hospital, but the reason is never mentioned.
    • He, Molly, and Penny Polar Bear are the only three kids in Peppa's playgroup who wear glasses.
    • Sometimes, he almost runs late for things like in School Bus Trip and Pedro is Late.
    • Sometimes, when traveling, he feels uneasy as shown in School Camp, The Train Ride, and Party Bus.
    • He is George's best friend-in-law as revealed in two episodes, which are The Eye Test and Mummy Rabbit's Bump.
    • As seen in Pedro the Cowboy, Pedro knows how to play the guitar.
    • Despite not being able to in Hoops, Pedro knows how to use a Hoop, as shown in Numbers.
    • His appearance bears a striking resemblance to Arthur Read from the Arthur books and TV series. Despite the two being different species.
